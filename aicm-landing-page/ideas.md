## Design Philosophy: Tech-Minimalist / Neo-Futurist

### Design Movement
**Minimalism meets Futurism** — combines clean, spacious layouts with high-end 3D motion graphics and neon accents to communicate sophistication and cutting-edge technology.

### Core Principles
1. **Premium Motion Over Static Content** — The 3D animation is the hero, not supporting decoration. It commands attention and communicates innovation.
2. **Restrained Color Palette** — Off-white/light grey base with purple/magenta neon and gold accents. Every color serves a purpose.
3. **Asymmetric Layout** — Text on left, 3D visuals on right. Creates visual tension and guides the eye naturally.
4. **Cinematic Quality** — Soft shadows, realistic lighting, particle effects, and subtle camera drift create depth and premium feel.

### Color Philosophy
- **Background:** Off-white/light grey (matte, clean, professional)
- **Accent Colors:** Purple and magenta neon (futuristic, tech-forward, energetic)
- **Highlight:** Gold coin (high contrast, precious, focal point)
- **Text:** Dark charcoal/black (readable, sophisticated)
- **Emotional Intent:** Trust (white), innovation (neon), value (gold)

### Layout Paradigm
**Asymmetric Split Layout:**
- Left 40%: Hero text (headline, sub-headline, CTA)
- Right 60%: 3D animated environment
- Top: Transparent navigation bar with logo, menu, and CTA button
- Creates visual balance through contrast, not symmetry

### Signature Elements
1. **Rolling Gold Coin** — Physics-based animation through carved 3D tracks
2. **Neon Lighting** — Purple/magenta glows on white surfaces, reflecting off the coin
3. **Particle Effects** — Floating bokeh dust particles adding atmosphere
4. **Subtle Camera Drift** — Slow, imperceptible panning motion preventing static feel

### Interaction Philosophy
- Navigation is minimal and transparent (doesn't distract from hero)
- CTA button is outlined (elegant, not aggressive)
- Hover states are subtle (respects the premium aesthetic)
- All motion is smooth and physics-based (feels natural, not jarring)

### Animation Guidelines
- **3D Scene:** Continuous looping animation with rolling coin, dynamic lighting, and particle effects
- **Camera Motion:** Very slow, almost imperceptible floating/panning
- **Lighting:** Soft, realistic shadows that shift with camera movement
- **Particles:** Gentle, floating bokeh dots across the screen
- **Transitions:** Smooth, physics-based (no abrupt cuts)

### Typography System
- **Font Family:** Modern sans-serif (Inter or similar geometric sans)
- **Headline:** Heavy bold weight (establish dominance)
- **Navigation/Body:** Regular or medium weight (sophisticated, airy)
- **Hierarchy:** Clear visual distinction through weight, not size alone

### Brand Essence
**Positioning:** The smarter, AI-powered decentralized marketplace for the future of commerce.
**Personality:** Sophisticated, innovative, forward-thinking, premium.

### Brand Voice
- Headlines: Bold, confident, future-focused
- CTAs: Clear, action-oriented, not pushy
- Microcopy: Elegant, professional, no generic filler

### Logo & Branding
- **Logo:** Purple geometric icon (abstract, tech-forward)
- **Wordmark:** "aicm" in clean sans-serif
- **Signature Color:** Purple/magenta neon

### Signature Brand Color
**Purple/Magenta Neon** — Unmistakably futuristic, energetic, and tech-forward. Used for accents and lighting effects.

## Style Decisions
- Navigation bar remains transparent to showcase the 3D scene
- Hero text is left-aligned for asymmetric visual interest
- 3D coin animation is the primary focal point and engagement driver
- Particle effects add subtle texture without overwhelming the design
- All animations are continuous and looping (no pause states)
